Mini Zucchini Pizzabites

Spice it up by learning how to make mini zucchini pizzas!

Ingredients

  • A large zucchini cut into 1/4 slices.
  • 1/8 salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/3 cup pizza sauce
  • Minced fresh basil
  • 3/4 cup shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • Your preferred topping in small size.

Instructions

  1. Preheat broiler. Organize your zucchini on a greased baking sheet.
  2. Broil 3-4 inches until it is crispy tender, 1-2 minutes per side.
  3. Scatter zucchini with salt and pepper. You may do so in the following order: The top with sauce, cheese, and your other preferred toppings (in mini size of course)
  4. Broil until cheese is melted, about 1 minute.
  5. Then, sprinkle with basil.
